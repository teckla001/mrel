APARTMENT FACILITIES


Parking area

The apartments have a variety of parking space depending on the area of the apartment. They have full security service and cctv camera monitoring 24/7 where your vehicles will be safe and secure.

Kitchen Space

The kitchen space will depend on the type of the apartment inquired but every apartment has an equally sized-fit kitchen with a well-grated sink and a dishwasher with equally-fit cabinets for all your kitchen accessories.

Bedroom Space

There are apartmentsthe are Two-bedrooms with a master room and another bedroom and some apartment with only a master room. Nevertheless, all the rooms have closets and can fit a queen-sized bed.
